Hey, I’m KD.

I'm a Computer Science graduate turned freelance web and software developer. While I spend most of my time building in the digital world, I created this space to build something more personal—a home for my words.

Poetry has always been my way of translating thoughts into meaning and emotions into something tangible. It’s where I feel most free. This page is my canvas, filled with honest reflections, quiet confessions, and moments that matter.

To help guide you through, I’ve organized the poems into different categories that reflect the heart behind each piece. Divine Poems explore themes of faith, hope, and spirituality, while Family Poems celebrate bonds that last forever and the deep connections that shape us. Special Poems are dedicated to those who inspire love and admiration, and Friendship Poems honor the joy, trust, and memories we share with the people who walk beside us. And for those looking to stumble across something unexpected, Random Poems offer a space to discover the unfiltered and the in-between.

Each poem is a small window into a larger story—sometimes mine, sometimes a feeling we all know. Whether you're here to reflect, to relate, or simply to read something real, I’m glad you’re here.

Welcome to my world of words.
