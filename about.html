Hey, I’m Kenzeus Drake

A Computer Science graduate turned freelance web and software developer. While I spend most of my time building in the digital world, I created this space to build something a little different: a home for my words.

Poetry has always been my way of translating thoughts into meaning, emotions into something tangible. It’s where I feel most free. This page is my personal canvas, filled with honest reflections, quiet confessions, and pieces of life that matter to me.

To help you explore, I’ve organized my poems into categories that reflect the heart behind each one:

Inspirational reflections on faith, hope, and spirituality.

Family Poems

Bonds that last forever, celebrating our closest connections.

Special Poems

Dedicated to those who inspire love and admiration.

Friendship Poems

Celebrating companionship and the journey shared with friends.

Random Poems

Discover something new from our diverse collection of themes.

Each poem is a small window into a bigger story—sometimes mine, sometimes universal. Whether you're here to feel, to reflect, or to simply read something real, I’m glad you made it.
