When God Sends Little Lights

Some days feel like walking through a fog, heavy with unspoken struggles and quiet battles no one sees.

I've been carrying the weight, wondering if anyone notices how hard it's been, feeling like I'm lost in the silence.

Then today—in the middle of all that weight—a message came.

From Mom. A screenshot. An appreciation post from someone I've helped, someone who's grown, someone I never imagined I'd impact this much.

She said, "Thank you, kuya."

And for a moment, my heart smiled. Even just for a moment, God reminded me:

You are seen. You are valued. You are not alone.

He didn't take the pain away—but He sent a spark of joy to carry me through it. Because that's who He is.

The God who finds a way to reach us right when we need it most.

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted, and saves those who are crushed in spirit." — Psalm 34:18

The Blooming Ache

The sakura is blooming again—soft, fragile, and full of life. But here I am, standing under its petals, carrying a heart that's missing someone.

It's strange, how life keeps going even when a piece of you feels stuck in yesterday.

I smile. I move. I show up. But inside, there's a silent ache blooming with every reminder of you.

A memory. A voice. A familiar place. And suddenly, I’m back to that moment—when you were here, and then, you weren’t.

But maybe that's what love does. It lingers. It aches. And it keeps blooming, even when the season has changed.

So I let the ache bloom. Not to drown in it, but to honor you.

Because love like that never dies—it just becomes a softer kind of presence.

"Blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted." — Matthew 5:4

In the Heart of Forever

Obasan, though you're no longer here,
Your love still echoes, crystal clear.
The whispers of your voice remain,
A song that heals, a soft refrain.

Where are you now, in heaven's light?
Do you feel my tears in the quiet of the night?
I miss your laugh, your gentle smile,
The way you'd hold me for a while.

Your wisdom like a river’s flow,
Still nourishes my heart and soul.
Why did you have to go so soon?
Could I have had just one more afternoon?

But in my heart, you live and stay,
Guiding me in every way.
Do you watch me as I grow and strive,
Encouraging me to keep alive?

Though I wish you were still here,
I trust God's plan, I have no fear.
You’ve taught me love, you've shown me grace,
Your lessons etched in time and space.

Obasan, you’re with angels now,
Though I long for you, I know somehow,
It’s His will that calls you near,
And for that, my heart feels clear.

Do you see how far I’ve come,
How every step still leads me home?
Your spirit lives in all I do,
In every prayer, I carry you.

I wanted you back, to hold your hand,
But I trust in God's divine command.
I thank the Lord for all you gave,
And for the love you taught me to save.

Obasan, you'll always be my guide,
In my heart, you will reside.
No time or place can change this fact,
Your love is here, and that’s a pact.

And though you’ve passed beyond this earth,
In my heart, you know your worth.
Forever cherished, forever dear,
Obasan, you’ll always be near.
