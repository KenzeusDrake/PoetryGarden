When God Sends Little Lights

Some days feel like walking through a fog,
Heavy with unspoken struggles,
And quiet battles no one sees—
I've been carrying the weight.

Wondering if anyone notices
How hard it's been, how lost I feel
In the silence, in the ache,
In the waiting that won't heal.

Then today—in the middle of all that weight—
A message came. From Mom. A screenshot.
An appreciation post from someone I'd helped—
Someone who's grown, someone I never imagined.

She said, "Thank you, kuya."
And for a moment, my heart smiled.
Even just for a moment, God reminded me:
You are seen. You are valued.

You are not alone.
He didn't take the pain away—
But He sent a spark of joy
To carry me through it. Because that's who He is.

The God who finds a way
To reach us right when we need it most.
With whispers, with moments,
With little lights that bring us home.

"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted, and saves those who are crushed in spirit."
— Psalm 34:18
