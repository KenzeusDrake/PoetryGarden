A True Friend’s Voice

(For the ones who show up, every time)

Not all heroes wear capes—some just answer the phone when no one else does.

They sit with your silence, laugh with your chaos, and hold your broken words like treasure.

A friend isn’t just company. It’s the mirror that reminds you who you are when the world forgets.

It’s the steady presence when storms rage, the hand on your back when you’re too tired to stand.

True friends don't just hear you—they listen between the lines, speak in pauses, and show up without needing to be asked.

If you have one, be thankful. If you are one, stay faithful. The world needs more hearts like that.
