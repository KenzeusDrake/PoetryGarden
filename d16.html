My Point of View: The Proof They Keep Ignoring

I saw a comment while scrolling on my socmed, “How do you know God is real?”
And honestly, I could give them a hundred reasons.

But here’s what I really want to say—
This is what I believe.
This is what I see.
This is what makes sense to both my mind and my soul.

The Universe Had a Beginning—And Beginnings Need a Beginner

Science says the universe began 13.8 billion years ago with the Big Bang.
That event was the start of time, space, and matter.
But even physicist Stephen Hawking admitted: "The universe has not always existed. It had a beginning."

And what causes a beginning?
According to the Law of Causality, every effect has a cause.
Something can’t come from nothing.
And the First Law of Thermodynamics? Energy can’t create itself. It can only be transferred.

So how did something come from nothing?
Only an eternal, immaterial, powerful force could exist outside time, matter, and space.

Genesis 1:1 — “In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth.”

The Universe Was Fine-Tuned for Life

Scientists call it the Anthropic Principle—
The idea that the universe appears deliberately designed for human life.

If gravity were slightly weaker, stars couldn’t form.
If it were stronger, stars would burn too fast.
If the Earth were just a little closer or farther from the sun, we’d freeze or burn.
Even the cosmological constant (which controls the expansion of the universe) is so precise, Stephen Weinberg said:
"Life as we know it would be impossible if it were off by one part in 10 to the 120th power."

That’s not random.

Isaiah 45:18 — “For this is what the Lord says—He who created the heavens… He did not create it to be empty, but formed it to be inhabited.”

DNA: The Language of God

Each of our cells carries a DNA code with over 3 billion letters—
A biological instruction manual.

Francis Collins, the leader of the Human Genome Project and a Christian, said:
"DNA is the language of God."

Because code requires intelligence.
Letters require a writer.
Messages need a sender.

Psalm 139:13-14 — “For you created my inmost being; you knit me together in my mother’s womb… I am fearfully and wonderfully made.”

That’s not poetry to me—it’s proof.

Life Can’t Come from Non-Life

The theory of evolution may explain change over time—
but it doesn’t explain how life started.

The Miller-Urey experiment tried to recreate early Earth conditions and “spark” life—
but it didn’t even come close to creating a living cell.

Even atheist scientists now admit:
"Abiogenesis (life from non-life) is still one of the biggest mysteries in biology."

Genesis 2:7 — “Then the Lord God formed a man from the dust of the ground and breathed into his nostrils the breath of life…”

Life comes from life— and the Author of life gave it.

Consciousness, Morality, and Meaning—Where Did They Come From?

We don’t just survive—we think, choose, create, worship.
But natural selection doesn’t need art, prayer, or sacrifice to survive.
So why do they exist?

Why do we hunger for justice, cry at funerals, sing with passion, or question the afterlife?

Ecclesiastes 3:11 — “He has also set eternity in the human heart…”

That’s why nothing in this world fully satisfies—
Because I was made for more.

Morality Isn’t Just a Human Idea—It’s a Divine Signature

If there’s no God, then morality is a cultural invention.
But no culture praises cowardice. No tribe admires betrayal.

Romans 2:15 — “The requirements of the law are written on their hearts, their consciences also bearing witness.”

We know some things are objectively wrong.
Not because we were taught—
But because we were designed that way.

Even Time Bows to the Eternal

Albert Einstein’s Theory of Relativity showed that time is not absolute.
It’s tied to matter and energy.
So if the universe had a beginning, time itself had a beginning.

That means the Cause of the universe must be timeless.

Revelation 1:8 — “I am the Alpha and the Omega… who is, and who was, and who is to come.”

He’s not bound by time. He holds it in His hands.

Jesus: The Invisible Made Visible

People say, “Show me God, then I’ll believe.”
But He already did.

John 1:14 — “The Word became flesh and made His dwelling among us.”

Jesus healed the sick. He calmed storms. He raised the dead. He forgave sins.
And He didn’t just die—He walked out of His own grave.

Over 500 eyewitnesses saw Him after the resurrection.
And early disciples gave their lives for that truth—
Not for a myth. Not for power.
But because they saw the Living God.

The Resurrection Changed History—And Me

If Jesus didn’t rise from the dead, there is no Christianity.
But if He did—
Then everything He said is true.
And that changes everything.

1 Corinthians 15:14 — “If Christ has not been raised, our preaching is useless and so is your faith.”

But the evidence, both historical and spiritual, keeps pointing to one thing:
He is alive.

And that’s why I believe not just in a God—
But in Jesus, the God who came close.

In the End, I Don’t Just Believe in God—He’s the Reason I Breathe

If there’s no God:
There’s no reason for beauty.
No foundation for truth.
No purpose in suffering.
No hope after death.

But I believe:

Acts 17:28 — “For in Him we live and move and have our being.”

Every atom, every heartbeat, every moment—
it all points back to Him.

So When They Ask Me: "Where’s the Proof?"

I say: Look at the stars. Look at DNA. Look at the human heart.
Look at the empty tomb. Look at the longing in your soul.

It all leads to one truth:
God is not just real—He is the reason for everything.
