IDK

What is this stirring in my chest,
that wakes me even when I rest?
A silent voice, a nameless fire,
not quite longing, not desire.

Why do I glance, then look away,
as if my eyes might go astray?
Why does your presence shift the air,
like something’s more when you are there?

When did the simplest things you do
start painting skies in deeper hue?
I never meant to feel this way—
but feelings bloom, they disobey.

Where in my soul does this begin?
A thread, a pull, a world within.
Not marked by lines or heavy signs,
just quiet thoughts between the lines.

Who am I in all of this?
A drifting echo, hit or miss?
The one who cheers but hides the ache,
a quiet leaf beneath your wake.

How do I hold what has no name?
No claim, no voice, no one to blame.
Just questions floating in my sea—
not asking you, but asking me.

I don’t expect, I don’t reveal,
I only ask what’s true, what’s real.
It isn't loud, but it exists—
I don't know what,
But it persists.
